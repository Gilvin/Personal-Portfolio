Game Development

Ever since I was a kid, I have always been drawn to playing games. It has brought me through the toughest times of my life and given be the biggest smiles. Gaming has connected me with so many amazing people that I still talk to today. I wanted to create stories that people can get not only get immersed in, but learn from. There's nothing like getting pulled into a game, and I've always felt using that pull to tell a story with a long-term thematic idea at it's core would make games better.

Web Development

Front-end web development is all about creativity and creative problem-solving. As a front-end web developer, you’ll have the skills to build customized websites to create the best possible user experience. Front-end web development is an exciting career because it’s always evolving and constantly changing. This means you’ll always have the opportunity to interact with new tools and learn new skills, keeping you engaged in your career. It’s a field that provides many opportunities for hands-on learning.

Mobile Development

With technology becoming more mobile oriented it is important to learn how to develop in multiple different platforms, making you a versitile engineer. With the growing number of people accessing the Internet via smartphones and tablets, mobile app development has the unique ability to access a large number of potential consumers. In essence, a mobile app allows you to have millions of new customers at your fingertips.
